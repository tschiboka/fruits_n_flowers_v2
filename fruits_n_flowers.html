Walkthrough

This is a Candy Crush‐style browser game, where you'll test your logic and problem‐solving skills against time.

Your goal is to place the flowers on the top of the baskets.

Flowers are moving only horizontally, but if a flower happens to be at the top of a basket parted by only one fruit, you will still be able to swipe it down on the basket.

You won't be able to move immobile characters like walls, stones, and baskets, they have fixed position on the game‐board.

In order to get the flowers on the basket, you'll need to destroy the fruits and crystals under it by matching at least three identical fruits in a row or a column.

Any time you create a match, the crystals around them are getting smaller to the point they disappear from the board, making the way clear to your flowers.

To complete a level, you need to be able to collect in your basket as many flowers as the current level requirement.

Make sure you play as quick and smart as possible, so you will not be running out of time!

Every level has their own set of points.

If you can collect the required amount of points, next time when you visit that level, your points will be doubled!

You are not required to max‐out a level in order to unlock the next one though.

In case you collected more flowers than the level requirement, you'll be rewarded with bonus gems.

Match any of the bonus fruits or gems, and they will trigger an explosion according to their shape, destroying every fruit and stone their way.

Swapping any two bonuses will combine their power!

Every time you collect the minimum level requirement of flowers, your points you made on that level will be added to the total points, which you can spend later in the Store.

In case you need a special gem, you can drop one from your inventory on any valid game‐board cell. (Just swipe it up!)

Don't find the special gem you just needed in your inventory? Feel free to hit the Store and create the one that you like.

Some levels are intentionally designed to be extremely difficult, so it is a good idea to spend some time on a lower level (ideally maxed out, don't forget: double points) to boost your total points and inventory.

Some levels are impossible to complete in time without deploying your arsenal of gems and bonuses from the inventory.

Sometimes you will find it difficult to spot out a good match (which will likely be the situation on higher levels), therefore the game gives you random hints in every 10 seconds of inactivity.

Why not boost your hints? Hit the shop, choose fast hints (3 seconds between hints) or best hints (gives you the most valuable hint on the board) or both!

You can always stop, pause, or restart the current level, just swipe the arrow at the bottom of the page for the main menu.

In case any help needed while you play, you can always revisit this quick walkthrough, your game will be paused!




Have Fun!
